Qualification to the 2025 FIVB Beach Volleyball World Championships

🏆 AVC Qualification Pathway to the 2025 FIVB Beach Volleyball World Championships

  • Max quota from this AVC pathway:
    • Men: 4 teams (8 athletes)
    • Women: 4 teams (8 athletes)
  • Qualification period: 6 Oct 2024 to 5 Oct 2025, includes all Beach Pro Tour events and AVC homologated tournaments.
  • Ranking is calculated by National Federation (NF):
    • Each NF may have up to 2 positions per gender:
      • NF1 = team with the best result in each event
      • NF2 = team with the second-best result in each event
    • Calculated from Top 4 AVC + Top 4 Beach Pro Tour results, regardless of players.
  • Results by athletes already qualified via FIVB Rankings including Host Country teams (as of 5 Oct 2025) are excluded.
  • Tie-breaks: best result at 2024 Asian Champs ➔ total points ➔ drawing lots.
